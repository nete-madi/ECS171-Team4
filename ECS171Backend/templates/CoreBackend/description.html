Project Description

Our website will allow doctors or patients to print out standardized data entry sheets for patient information entry. After the doctor ( or the patient themselves) has inputted the patient’s information (via pen/pencil on the printout), the website will read the sheet and decipher the written text. This text is then converted into a CSV file which put into the hospital’s data record for that patient or stored for more processing later on.

This website allows patients to easily communicate their symptoms and personal information to their doctors during an era of predominantly online communication. By allowing both the patient and the doctor to fill out the information gives the flexibility of receiving this information without wasting the time of the professionals. This is also a more accurate way to document the patients information without miscommunications, such as an interrupt in your zoom meeting.
